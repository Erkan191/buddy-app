How Buddy Matcher Works

Follow these simple steps to create random, fair buddy groups in seconds:

  • Enter Participants: Type or paste the names of all participants into the input box above. You can copy entire columns straight from your spreadsheet!
  • Select Group Size: Choose the number of people per group using the dropdown menu. Buddy Matcher will handle leftover participants automatically.
  • Generate Matches: Click the Generate Buddy Match button. Your random groups will appear instantly in the output box below.
  • Copy & Share: Easily copy your generated groups or share them with your team. No participant is left out.
