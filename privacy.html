Privacy

Buddy Matcher is a simple browser-based tool. The names you enter are stored in your browser using local storage so the tool can remember them on your device.

What the tool stores

The tool may store names, saved lists and your most recent grouping in your browser on your own device.

What the tool does not do

This static version of Buddy Matcher does not send your names to a backend server for matching.

Contact

If you contact me directly by email, I will see the information you choose to send in that email.

Third-party links

This site may link to third-party services such as Buy Me a Coffee, Stripe or Gumroad. Those services have their own privacy policies and terms.

Changes

I may update this page from time to time if the site changes.
