About Buddy Up

Buddy Up is a simple tool designed to randomly generate buddy pairings for teams, classrooms, and events. Our mission is to make collaboration fun and easy by removing the hassle of manual pairing. Whether for school, work, or social groups, Buddy Up helps bring people together.

About Me

I’m a dad of two young children with a full-time job that keeps me busy from morning to night. Yet, in the evenings after the kids are asleep, I carve out time to teach myself app development—one line of code at a time. I started this journey to escape the rat race and build a life where I’m more in control of my time and able to focus on what truly matters: family, friends, and all the things I love. This app was born from a simple idea I had while watching a colleague draw names from a hat during a team building day. I thought, “There’s got to be a smarter, more fun way of doing that!” and that's when BuddyUp was born. It’s my first step towards creating tools that make life easier and more enjoyable for others. Whatever you use it for, I hope you enjoy using it as much as I enjoyed making it!
